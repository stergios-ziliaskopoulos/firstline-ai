The Problem With Other AI Support Tools

They're incentivised to trap users, not resolve them.

Most AI support tools charge per resolution. That creates a direct incentive to keep users stuck rather than hand off cleanly. Deflection gets rewarded. Your customer's experience doesn't.

The TrustQueue Approach

TrustQueue is not designed to maximise deflection. It is designed to maximise trust. It answers only from your documentation. When confidence is low, it stops and routes to a human. Automatically, every time. Human handoff is a core product feature, not a premium add-on.

Why TrustQueue is built differently

Grounded Answers Only

TrustQueue only retrieves answers from documentation you've approved. If the answer isn't there, it won't fabricate one. It routes to a human instead.

Clean Human Handoff

When confidence drops below your threshold, TrustQueue stops, collects the customer's email, and notifies your team with the full conversation transcript. Human handoff is a core feature, not a premium upsell.

Step 1

Send us your documentation

Share your FAQs, SOPs, help center articles, or Notion pages. We audit the content, clean it up where needed, and build a knowledge base your AI is authorized to use.

Step 2

Every answer is grounded and cited

Each question is matched against your documentation with a confidence score. If confidence is high, TrustQueue answers and cites the source. If not, it stops and routes to a human.
